What Exactly are NFTs?

Many of the world’s most valuable assets, like rare diamonds and original masterpieces like the Mona Lisa, are as valuable as they are because they’re non-fungible — irreplaceable with no true equivalent. Similar to cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, NFTs are digital assets that can be purchased, sold, and traded at will, just as you could else you owned. As you might surmise from the word “token,” NFTs are indeed tokenized. This means that each one is unique and unlike any other, including other versions of the asset the NFT is based on.
